Understanding HSL Color Model

A concise guide with examples & reference

What is HSL?

HSL stands for Hue, Saturation, and Lightness. It is a modern and intuitive way to define colors in CSS.

  • Hue: the color type (0–360 degrees on the color wheel).
  • Saturation: intensity of color (0% gray to 100% full color).
  • Lightness: brightness (0% black, 50% normal, 100% white).

hsl(0, 100%, 50%) – Red

hsl(120, 100%, 40%) – Green

hsl(240, 100%, 50%) – Blue
